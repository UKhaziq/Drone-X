Who We Are?

Founded in 2023, a team of two from Penang Free School (PFS) has developed this website to promote DRONE X, an all-in-one drone solutions provider that simplifies task across industry of agriculture through modernisation, cost effective and latest technology of IR 4.0. This website explains numerous advantages of our high-tech drones in assisting and facilitating daily agricultural activities to be easier and more efficient. We proudly promote seven drone models, which are Emolian, DJI, UAI, Tello, Parrots, MJX and OnZ. All models have their own advantages and specialities according to price and specification. DRONE X takes you to the whole new level through a holistic ecosystem beyond our core drone capabilities. Representing Generation-Z, we are the young pioneers of the next generation who are advancing the latest technology of drone.

The Advantages of Drones in Agriculture

The core advantages of drones in agriculture is to increase the efficiency of productivity. For instance, drones can cover large area of farmland in efficient manner. Furthermore, drones’ usage may reduce labour workers, and subsequently reduce costs. Additionally, drones ‘educate’ farmers to identify problems in advance by providing information about crops. Thus, farmers can demonstrate preventive actions. In short, drones are the future of agricultural industry.
